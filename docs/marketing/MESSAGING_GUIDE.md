# Messaging Guide

> Consistent language for all Aivyx communications.

## Core Messages

### Tagline
> **Own Your Intelligence**

### Elevator Pitch (10 seconds)
> Aivyx is a privacy-first AI agent that runs on your machine, remembers your context, and keeps your data encrypted.

### Short Description (30 seconds)
> Aivyx is an open-source AI agent built for professionals who care about privacy. It runs locally, supports multiple LLM providers, has encrypted persistent memory, and connects to external tools via MCP. Your data never leaves your machine unless you choose it to.

### Long Description (60 seconds)
> Most AI tools require you to hand your data to a cloud provider. Aivyx takes a different approach: it runs entirely on your machine, encrypts everything at rest, and gives you a tamper-proof audit log of every action. It supports Ollama for fully local inference, or external providers like OpenAI and Anthropic when you need more power. With 22 built-in skills, MCP tool connectivity, and a beautiful terminal UI, Aivyx is the AI agent that works for you — not the other way around.
